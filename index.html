The Neurovascular & Endovascular Centre is focused on improving advanced brain and stroke treatment services in Bangladesh. We provide minimally invasive neuro-interventional care using modern medical technology and precise clinical methods. Our team supports patients facing critical conditions such as stroke and brain hemorrhage with fast and effective management. We are committed to safe, ethical, and high-quality care to enhance recovery and save lives.

Neurointervention & Neurology expertises

Mechanical Thrombectomy (Stroke)

Urgent endovascular clot retrieval to restore blood flow and reduce disability in acute ischemic stroke.

Aneurysm Coiling & Stenting

Minimally invasive coiling and stent-assisted remodeling to secure brain aneurysms and prevent rupture.

Diagnostic Cerebral Angiography

High-resolution imaging of cerebral vessels to evaluate AVMs, aneurysms, stenosis, and dissections.

Carotid & Intracranial Stenting

Endovascular stent placement to treat critical stenosis and reduce stroke risk.

Comprehensive Neurology

aManagement of epilepsy, movement disorders, headaches, neuropathies, and neurodegenerative diseases.

CSF & Neurodiagnostics

Lumbar puncture and neurodiagnostic assessments for infections, inflammatory, and demyelinating diseases.

AVM / DAVF Embolization

Advanced endovascular embolization of arteriovenous malformations (AVMs) and dural arteriovenous fistulas (DAVFs) to reduce abnormal blood flow and prevent neurological complications.

Indirect Carotid-Cavernous Fistula (CCF)

Minimally invasive treatment of carotid-cavernous fistulas using endovascular techniques to restore normal blood circulation and relieve ocular and neurological symptoms.

Flow Diverter Assisted Brain Artery Reconstruction

Flow-diverter stent technology for reconstruction of diseased cerebral arteries and treatment of complex intracranial aneurysms while preserving normal blood flow
